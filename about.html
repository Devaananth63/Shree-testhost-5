Our Profile

Shree Gokul Shipping Services, based in Tuticorin, Tamil Nadu, is a trusted name in the maritime and logistics industry. With over a decade of experience, we specialize in providing reliable, cost-effective shipping and freight solutions across India and beyond.

We offer a wide range of services including freight forwarding, customs clearance, warehousing, inland transportation, and end-to-end cargo handling. Our team ensures smooth coordination, timely deliveries, and complete transparency throughout the shipping process.

Strategically located near one of India’s key ports, we leverage technology and local expertise to streamline global trade. From small businesses to large enterprises, we serve clients with a commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.
